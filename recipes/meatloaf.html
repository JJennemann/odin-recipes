Meatloaf

Description

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all and it's quite good!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp prepared mustard
  • 1/3 cup of ketchup

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk, and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly gread 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
